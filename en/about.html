Eva Margon Štanta works in the fields of illustration, design and animation. She obtained her Master's degree in Illustration at the Academy of Fine Arts and Design in Ljubljana in 2019, and further developed her skills during a study exchange at Minerva Art Academy in Groningen, the Netherlands. In addition to book illustration, she focuses strongly on scientific, popular science and natural history illustration, while also exploring reduction linocut in her graphic studio. Whenever possible, she transforms illustrations into short animated sequences. Her projects most often intertwine with animal and botanical motifs.
