Eva Margon Štanta works in the fields of illustration, design and animation. She completed a Master’s degree in Illustration at ALUO in 2019 and continued developing her practice during a study exchange at Minerva Academy in Groningen, the Netherlands. Alongside book illustration, she focuses on scientific, popular science and natural history illustration, and in her print studio she explores reduction linocut. When the opportunity arises, she turns illustrations into sequences of animated frames. Her projects are most often interwoven with animal and botanical motifs.
